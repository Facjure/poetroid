DESOLATE and lone	
All night long on the lake	
Where fog trails and mist creeps,	
The whistle of a boat	
Calls and cries unendingly,	        
Like some lost child	
In tears and trouble	
Hunting the harbor’s breast	
And the harbor’s eyes.
