Hearts, like doors, will ope with ease
To very, very little keys,
And don't forget that two of these
Are "I thank you" and "If you please."
Come when you're called,
Do what you're bid,
Close the door after you,
Never be chid.
Seldom "can't,"
Seldom "don't;"
Never "shan't,"
Never "won't."
