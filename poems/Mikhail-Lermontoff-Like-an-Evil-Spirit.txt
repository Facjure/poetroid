Like an evil spirit have you
  Shocked my heart from out its rest,
If you'll take it quite away now—
  You wil win my healing blest!
My heart thy temple evermore!
  Your face,—the altar's Godhead sign!
Not heaven's grace,—your smiles, restore,
  Grant absolution, joy divine!
